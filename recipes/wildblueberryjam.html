Wild Blueberry Jam

You open smile and feel energy coming from it aroma, many have confessed, when you spread it the taste triples, get it and be amazed.

Ingredients

Steps

  1. 4 Cups of Wild Blueberries
  2. 1 Cup Spring Water
  3. 2 Tablespoons Sea Moss Gel
  4. Your Choice of Sweetener
  5. 1/2 Cup Agave
  6. 1 1/2 Cup Blended Dates/Figs or Pastes to taste

Directions

  • Pules ingredients in blender, bring water to light boil,
    And occasionally stir for 10-20 mins of desired thickness.
  • let cool on the fridge until cold.

  • Tip:
      May use recipe to for strawberry and apple jam as well,
      to make thicker do not blend or pules.
