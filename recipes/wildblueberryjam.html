Wild Blueberry Jam Recipes

Ingredients

Steps

  1. 4 Cups of Wild Blueberries
  2. 1 Cup Spring Water
  3. 2 Tablespoons Sea Moss Gel
  4. Your Choice of Sweetener
  5. 1/2 Cup Agave
  6. 1 1/2 Cup Blended Dates/Figs or Pastes to taste

Directions

Pules ingredients in blender, bring water to light boil
and occasionally stir for 10-20 mins of desired thickness. let cool on the fridge until cold.

Tip:
May use recipe to for strawberry and apple jam as well, to make thicker do not blend or pules.
